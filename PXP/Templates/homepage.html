RISK FREE AND FUNDING FREE PART EXCHANGE

Volume House Builders

With no differential required, our part exchange services allow you to part exchange properties outside of your usual criteria and operating area. De-risk your part exchange completely and continue to use part exchange when your own funding has been used up, helping volume housebuilders to meet their half year and year-end demands.

See more info
Small/Medium Builders

Our part exchange service gives small to medium sized housebuilders the ability to compete with volume housebuilders who already offer part exchange. With no funding required and no risks involved, part exchange guarantees sales and completions on time, every time.

See more info
Retirement House Builders

Part exchange provides a fast, stress free solution for your buyers who are retiring and downsizing. Our friendly team carry out a home visit as standard to thoroughly explain the process prior to any part exchange offer, and we allow your buyers to remain in their old property for two weeks to ease the stresses of the moving process.

See more info
New Homes Agents

Enhance your services to your new homes buyers by offering flexible part exchange through the PX Partnership. With no cost to you, no tie-ins and no price differential required, offering part exchange guarantees sales and helps your clients to reach their financial targets.
