About the PX Partnership

Formed in 1984, The PX Partnership began life is ARC Relocation helping thousands of employees of corporate companies and goverment departments move home.

Learn More

ARC Relocation went on to become the largest independent Relocation Company in the UK with offices in London, Newbury, Manchester and Edinburgh. We were able to offer a unique level of service that attracted clients such as BP, Cadbury, Tesco, Standard Life, Pfizer and Barclays.

Due to our success and experience gained in the UK property market ARC Property Group was formed and further companies were added to the group. In 1997 we pioneered an innovative Part Exchange and Chain Breaking Scheme for the Retirement Homes Industry. This scheme enabled builders and developers of retirement properties to sell new homes using Part Exchange, yet without exposing them to any financial risk. As the business evolved we introduced similar schemes for the mainstream New Homes industry. Part Exchange is now our core business and so to reflect this, ARC Property Group rebranded to become The PX Partnership in January 2013.

Part of the BDZ Holdings group of companies, The PX Partnership has grown to become one of the leading Part Exchange providers and Property Purchase experts in the UK.
